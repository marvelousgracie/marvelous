ABOUT ME

Hello and welcome! Explore my page to learn a bit about who I am. Feel free to take a look around!

Hi! I'm Marvelous!

I was born in Mandaluyong City on September 11, 2003. Right now, I'm in my second year studying Information Technology at Rizal Technological University.

Growing up in Mandaluyong has been exciting. From the lively streets to the diverse community, Mandaluyong is not just a place on the map, it's a part of who I am and I'm excited about where it leads. But life is more than just classes, I find joy everytime I spend time with my family and friends, especially when we go out, it keeps me balanced and happy

In my free time, I enjoy reading novels, for me it is a portal to different worlds. From romance and fantasy, each novel is a journey, not just entertainment but insights that enrich my perspective. Of course I also watch movies and anime, each becomes experience, influencing not only my entertainment choices but also my appreciation for storytelling. Also, writing stories is not just a hobby, it's a journey of exploration. Whether crafting fictional characters, places or expressing my thoughts, writing serves as a canvas for my creativity. As well as practicing digital arts that allows me to express myself visually. From graphic design to exploring new tools and techniques, this creative outlet adds a colorful dimension to my interests.

"Life is my sketchbook. From classes to time with loved ones, it's the simple joys and diverse experiences that draw my unique story. Whether lost in novels, movies, or creating through writing and digital arts, each adds a splash of color to the pages of my journey."
